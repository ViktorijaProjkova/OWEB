Audi A6 2018

23200 EURO - The 2018 Audi A6 gets new standard equipment on the base Premium trim level including keyless entry/start and the MMI interface with navigation. Premium Plus models gets LED headlights as standard while the range-topping Prestige grade now comes with a 360-degree camera system. Cars with the optional V-6 now have more power and can be had with the Competition package, which adds black side mirror covers, red brake calipers, 20-inch alloy wheels, sport-tuned suspension, Quattro sport differential, aluminum interior trim, S Sport seats, and a three-spoke flat-bottom steering wheel with paddle shifters.Performance wise, the A6 was available with two engines: an entry level 2.0-liter 4-cylinder turbocharged engine developing 252 hp and the more powerful 3.0 6-cylinder supercharged powerplant with 340 hp. Both engines were mated with an 8-speed automatic transmission.
